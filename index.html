About the Tampa Streetcar Map

This application is part of a larger Tampa Through Time initiative that aims to make library collections more accessible by bringing them to light through a spatial lens. The map used for this project is a 1940 City of Tampa transit map depicting public transportation routes and is available for download through the University of South Florida Digital Collections webpage.

Citation

MacDonald Printing Company, inc., Tampa, Fla., "Tampa, Florida" (1940). Hillsborough County and Tampa Map Collection. Image 8.https://digitalcommons.usf.edu/fl_maps_local/8
